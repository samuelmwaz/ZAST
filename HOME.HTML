Project Unified

One Ecosystem. Every Layer.

A unified digital ecosystem combining transport, employment, and financial systems — purpose-built for Zambia and designed to scale across Africa.

The Opportunity

Why We Must Do This in Zambia

Informal Economy Dominance
Much of the economy relies on unverified vendors and informal trust networks that lack transparency — a gap ZAST directly addresses.
Cash Leakage in Transport
Public transport relies heavily on cash, leading to revenue loss for owners — solved with QR ticketing and real-time tracking.
Employment Gap
High unemployment among skilled workers despite demand for verified labor. The Job Connect closes this gap with guaranteed interviews and tiered access.
Mobile Money Growth
Rapid adoption of mobile money in Zambia provides the foundation for the Reflecting Balance digital wallet, enabling financial inclusion at scale.

Strategic Roadmap
