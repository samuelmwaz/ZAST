AutoRelief

Your Daily Commute, Reimagined

No more fumbling for exact change. AutoRelief turns your phone into your transport wallet — tap, ride, and go. For bus owners, it means knowing exactly what's been collected, in real time, every trip.

FOR COMMUTERS
Scan & Ride
Scan a QR code at any AutoRelief-enabled bus stop or on board. Your Reflecting Balance wallet is charged instantly — no cash, no queues, no disputes.
FOR TRANSPORT STAFF
Your Phone Is Your Till
ZAST provides smartphones for ticket validation. Every transaction is logged — meaning your work history builds automatically, opening doors to better employment opportunities within the platform.
FOR BUS OWNERS
Real-Time Revenue Accountability
See every fare collected on every route — live. Verified mechanics via the platform flag early faults before they become expensive repairs. Insurance and fuel delivery included in the ecosystem.

Project Unified

One Ecosystem. Every Layer.

ZAST is building a unified digital ecosystem combining transport, employment, and financial systems into one scalable platform — purpose-built for Zambia and designed to scale across Africa.

The Opportunity

Why We Must Do This in Zambia

Informal Economy Dominance
Much of the economy relies on unverified vendors and informal trust networks that lack transparency — a gap ZAST directly addresses.
Cash Leakage in Transport
Public transport is heavily reliant on cash, leading to revenue loss and security risks for owners — solved with QR ticketing and real-time tracking.
Employment Gap
High unemployment exists among skilled workers despite desperate need for verified labor. The Job Connect closes this gap with guaranteed interviews and tiered access.
Mobile Money Growth
Rapid adoption of mobile money in Zambia provides the foundation for the Reflecting Balance digital wallet ecosystem, enabling financial inclusion at scale.
